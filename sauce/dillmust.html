Dill-Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup light sour cream
  • 1/2 cup spicy brown or Dijon mustard
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh dill

Directions

In small bowl, whisk together sour cream and mustard until well mixed. Stir in dill. Transfer to small serving bowl. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Makes: 1 cup

Accompanies Broiled Salmon with Dill
