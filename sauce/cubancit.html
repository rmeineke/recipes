Cuban Citrus Rub

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion 4 garlic cloves zest of 2 limes zest of 1 large orange 1 tsp oregano salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Mince garlic and onion finely, add zest, oregano, and salt and pepper. Coat meat and cook .
