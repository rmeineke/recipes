Emeril’s Creole Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 tbs paprika
  • 2 tbs salt
  • 2 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tbs black pepper
  • 1 tbs onion powder
  • 1 tbs cayenne
  • 1 tbs dried oregano
  • 1 tbs dried thyme

Directions

Stir all ingredients together in a bowl.
