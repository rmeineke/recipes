Valery's Lemony Vinaigrette

1/3 cup olive oil
juice of 1/2 lemon
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
freshly ground pepper to taste
1-2 cloves of garlic - crushed
1 tablespoon fresh OR 1 teaspoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dijon mustard


mix altogether
