Honey/Soy Pork Roast Marinade

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce

Directions

Mix well. Use as marinade for roasted pork loin.
