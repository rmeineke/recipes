Yogurt and Cilantro Sauce


Ingredients:

    ___ 1/4 cup leeks, finely chopped
    ___ 3 tablespoons olive oil
    ___ 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    ___ 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
    ___ 1 cup yogurt
    ___ Salt and pepper to taste
    ___ Lemon juice (optional)

Directions:

Over medium heat, cook the chopped leeks in oil for 5
minutes. Add cilantro and mint and cook for 1 minute
more.

Remove the pan from the heat and whisk in the yogurt.
Season with salt and pepper and add lemon juice if
desired. Serve warm at once or refrigerate and serve
cold.

Makes: 1 1/2 cups

Can be used as a dip for cold fish, poultry or raw
vegetables.
