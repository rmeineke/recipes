Melanie's Pistachio Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shelled dry roasted, salted pistachios
  • 1/3 cup tarragon-flavored vinegar
  • 2/3 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • Dash of garlic powder, paprika and white pepper

Directions

Put all ingredients into a blender and puree to desired consistency. Serve chilled and use sparingly.
