Valery’s Lemony Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic - crushed
  • 1 tablespoon fresh OR 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
