Rice-Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

    Dressing:
    ___ 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    ___ 1/3 cup white wine vinegar
    ___ 2 tbl dry sherry
    ___ 1 1/2 tbl soy sauce
    ___ 3/4 tsp ground ginger
    ___ 1 clove minced garlic
    Combine ingredients in a jar and shake well.

    Salad:
    ___ 3 cups rice, cooked and hot
    ___ 2 cups packed, fresh torn spinach
    ___ 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
    ___ 1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds, toasted
    ___ 1/3 cup sliced green onions

Directions:

    Toss hot, cooked rice with dressing. Chill several hours. Add remaining ingredients and toss.
