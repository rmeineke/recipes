Southwestern Turkey Salad with Salsa


Ingredients:

    ___ 1 package (1 pound) ground turkey
    ___ 1- 2 oz jar salsa
    ___ 2 zucchini, sliced 1/8" and blanched
    ___ 2 avocados, sliced into wedges, coated with lemon
    ___ 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
    ___ 16 pitted ripe olives
    ___ 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
    ___ Blue corn chips or tortilla chips

Directions:

To blanch zucchini slices, place zucchini in boiling water
for 1 minute, then rinse with cold water

In 10" skillet, crumble ground turkey; cook over medium
heat until meat is no longer pink (10 mins). Stir in
salsa, continue cooking until salsa is heated through. On
each of the 4-6 dinner plates, arrange blanched zucchini
slices around inside rim of plate. Spoon over turkey
mixture. Arrange 5 avocado wedges across turkey.
Sprinkle with cilantro, olives and cheese. Serve with blue
corn chips.

Makes: 4-6 servings.
