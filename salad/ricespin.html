Rice Spinach Salad

Ingredients

  • Make several hours ahead.
  • Salad:
  • 3 cups cooked rice, HOT
  • 2 cups packed, torn, fresh spinach
  • 1 (11 ounce) can Mandarin oranges
  • 1/2 cup blanched, slivered almonds, toasted
  • 1/3 cup green onion, chopped
  • Dressing:
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tbs dry sherry
  • 1 1/2 tbs soy sauce
  • 3/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Directions

Combine all dressing ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid and shake well.

Cook rice per package directions. Toss hot, cooked rice with dressing and chill for several hours. Add remaining ingredients and toss.
