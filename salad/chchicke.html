Chinese Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 5 chicken breasts
  • 1 head lettuce
  • 2 oz rice sticks
  • 1 bundle green onion
  • 1 package sliced almonds
  • Dressing:
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons Accent (MSG)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds

Directions

Cut chicken into small pieces, boil or fry and cool.

Slice lettuce very thinly.

Deep fry the rice sticks. (It only takes a few seconds)

Chop green onion.

Roast almonds and cool.

I usually make twice the dressing. Put together at last minute so everything doesn't get soggy.
