Nine Layer Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head Iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 can water chestnuts, chopped
  • 1 package frozen peas, drained and dried well
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, crumbled
  • 1 pound of bacon, crisp and crumbled (or 1 jar real bacon pieces)
  • 2 or 3 tomatoes, chopped and drained.

Directions

Layer the first five ingredients in order, being sure that the mayonnaise forms a seal. Cover and let sit overnight. The next day, add the remainder of the ingredients in order listed and serve.
