Old Gorge Baked Apples

Ingredients

  • 6 green apples
  • 2 cups maple syrup
  • (Mix the following thoroughly)
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1/3 cup "Red Hots" candies
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions

Core apples but do not peel.

Stuff each apple with brown sugar mixture. Place in an oiled baking pan. Baste with maple syrup.

Bake at 350° for 40 minutes, or until apples are tender.
