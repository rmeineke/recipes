Magic Cookie Bars

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • 1 cup (6 oz) chocolate chips
  • 1 1/3 cup (3 1/2 oz can) flaked coconut
  • 1 can Eagle brand sweetened condensed milk

Directions

Layer ingredients as listed in 9x13 baking pan. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes. Cool slightly before cutting.
