Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons butter melted
  • 2 cups milk chocolate for topping

Directions

Mix and spread into ungreased 9x13 baking pan.

Topping

2 cups milk chocolate, melted, poured over mixture.

Refrigerate at least 2 hours. Let stand 30 minutes at room temperature before cutting so chocolate softens a bit.
