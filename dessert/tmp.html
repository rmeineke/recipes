White Chocolate Cheesecake


Ingredients:

    ___ 2 pounds cream cheese, room temperature
    ___ 14 ounces white chocolate (grated)
    ___ 6 egg yolks
    ___ 4 egg whites, whipped
    ___ 1/2 cup sugar
    ___ 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

Beat the cream cheese, add yolks and chocolate. Finally,
beat in the sugar and vanilla, don't overbeat. Fold in
the egg whites.

For the crust, use chocolate wafers, grind them up with a
little butter. Or use graham cracker type too. For the
finish, whip some cream (no sugar) and "frost" the cake.
Then sprinkle the whole thing with grated white chocolate.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes in a spring form
pan.
