Dreamsicle Cookies

Dry ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Tang instant breakfast powder
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour mixed with
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cup vanilla chips
  • 1 stick (1/4 lb) butter or margarine - VERY SOFT
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Mix dry ingredients well.

Add the butter, egg and vanilla.

Mix until completely blended. Add vanilla chips, then finish mixing with your hands.

Shape into balls the size of walnuts. Place 2" apart on a sprayed baking sheet.

Bake at 375 for 12-14 minutes until tops are very lightly browned. Cool for 5 minutes before removing.
