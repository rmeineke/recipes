Banana Split Cake

Ingredients:

    ___ 5 bananas, sliced
    ___ 3 sticks butter
    ___ 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
    ___ 2 eggs
    ___ 2 cups powdered sugar
    ___ 1 cup Cool Whip
    ___ 1 (18 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:

    Melt 1 cube of butter and add to graham cracker crumbs for crust. Pat into a 13x9x2-inch pan. Spread banana slices evenly over crust. Beat sugar, eggs and butter for 15 minutes, spread over bananas. Spread pineapple over top. Spread Cool Whip over top. Let sit overnight.
