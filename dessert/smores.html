Smores

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup light corn syrup
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • 1-11.5 ounce package milk chocolate morsels
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1-12 ounce package Golden Grahams cereal
  • 3 cups miniature marshmallows
  • * Yogurt chips optional

Directions

Grease rectangular pan, 13 x 9 x 2"

Heat syrup, margarine and morsels to boiling, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla.

Pour over cereal in bowl; toss until coated. Fold in marshmallows, 1 cup at a time. *Add yogurt chips here if desired* Press in pan with buttered back of spoon. Let stand 1 hour.

Cut into 2" squares. Store loosely covered at room temperature up to 2 days. Makes 24 squares.
