Pound Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 cubes butter, at room temp.
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • Use any two of these:
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp lemon extract
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup 7-Up
  • 3 cups Soft As Silk cake flour

Directions

Preheat oven to 350.

Cream butter and sugar very well, until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time. Add extracts and 7-Up. Add flour, 1/4 cup at a time, blending well between additions.

Spoon into a greased and floured large bundt pan. Bake for 1 hour and 10-20 minutes. Check often.
