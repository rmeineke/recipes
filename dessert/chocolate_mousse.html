Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 3 cups whipping cream

Directions

Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until fluffy and the sugar is dissolved. Beat in cocoa until dissolved. Whip cream until peaks form. Fold in half the whipped cream to the chocolate mixture. Fold in the second half. Refrigerate.
