Butter Crusts

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup (1/4 lb) butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 large egg yolk

Ingredients

In a food processor or bowl, combine flour and sugar. Add butter; whirl or rub in with your fingers until fine crumbs form. Add yolk; whirl or mix with a fork until dough holds together. Pat into a ball.

Divide dough into 6 equal portions. Firmly and evenly press each one over bottom and up sides of a 4 1/2-inch tart pan with removeable rim. Set pans slightly apart on a 14- by 17-inch baking sheet.

Bake in a 300° oven until pale gold, 15 to 25 minutes; let cool at least 15 minutes.

Used as crusts for Lime Tarts w/ Coconut Cream
