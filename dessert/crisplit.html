Crisp Little Lemon Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg Pillsbury Moist Supreme Lemon Cake Mix
  • 1 cup Rice Krispies cereal
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°.

Stir all ingredients together. Shape into balls and flatten slightly on cookie sheet. Bake for 9-10 minutes. Let cool on pan for several minutes then transfer to wire rack. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
