Best Banana Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 6 very ripe bananas
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • nuts optional

Ingredients

Mix soda with milk and eggs and let stand. Cream butter and sugar. Add bananas. Add alt. flour and milk mixture. Add vanilla and nuts. Bake 30-35 minutes at 350" in 3 nine inch pans.

Frost with cream cheese frosting.
