Norwegian Apple Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 medium cooking apples, cored and chopped
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Vegetable cooking spray
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions

Combine first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl; mix well. Add apple stirring lightly to coat.

Combine egg and vanilla; add to apple mixture, stirring just until dry ingredients are moistened. Spoon mixture into a 9-inch pie plate coated with cooking spray. Arrange almonds as desired on top.

Bake at 350° for 30 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve plain or with cool whip.
