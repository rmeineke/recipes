Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup margarine or shortening
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 crushed ripe bananas
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Ingredients

Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs and bananas. Then add remaining ingredients. Bake 1 hour 15 minutes at 350° in a greased, floured loaf pan.
