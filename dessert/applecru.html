Apple Crumb

Ingredients

  • 5 to 6 cups of sliced apples (about 6 apples)
  • 1 1/4 cups Bisquick
  • 2/3 cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Cinnamon

Ingredients

Heat oven to 350. Grease a square or rectangular baking dish and arrange apples in it. Mix bisquick, sugar and egg until crumbly. Sprinkle over apples. Drizzle the melted butter over it and sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake until topping is golden brown (30-35 mins).

It is generally a good idea to make twice as much crumb mixture.
