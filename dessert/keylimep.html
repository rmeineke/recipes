Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pints sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2/3 cup lime juice (from the Keys)
  • 1/2 fresh whipped cream

Ingredients

Beaten egg yolks and milk are gradually added to the key lime juice while stirring, a 9" pre-baked pie shell is filled with filling, store in refrigators.
