Snicker Doodles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soft shortening
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cup sifted flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar mixed with 2 tablespoons cinnamon

Directions

Mix shortening, sugar and eggs. Add flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Chill. Roll into walnut-sized balls and roll through sugar/cinnamon mixture. Bake 2" apart on ungreased cookie sheet at 400° - until golden, but soft - approx 8-10 minutes.
