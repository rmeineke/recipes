Chocolate Chip Kahlua Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 chocolate cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small package instant vanilla pudding
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 pound sour cream
  • 1 - 6 oz package chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup Kahlua

Directions

Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour one Bundt pan. Mix all ingredients except chocolate chips in large mixing bowl. Mix well. Spoon in chocolate chips. Spoon mixture into Bundt pan. Bake 45-50 mins. Cool for 5-10 minutes them remove from pan. Dust with powdered sugar. Let cool completely.
