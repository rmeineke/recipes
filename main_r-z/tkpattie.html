Turkey Patties w/ Dill-Cucumber Sauce

Ingredients

  • Turkey Patties:
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/3 cup fresh bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Cucumber Sauce:
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon dill weed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

In medium bowl, mix all the turkey pattie ingredients. Shape into 4 or 6 patties, 3/4" thick. In a 10" skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium high heat. Place the patties in oil. Cook for 5 to 6 mins per side or until browned.

Meanwhile, in a medium bowl stir together all the sauce ingredients. Serve sauce over patties.

Makes: 4 to 6
