Spaghetti Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 lb lean ground beef or turkey
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 large garlic clove, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup cottage or ricotta cheese

Directions

Crust: Cook spaghetti and drain. Stir in 2 tbs butter, 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese and two well-beaten eggs into the drained spaghetti. Spoon mixture into a 9-inch pie plate, pressing to form crust. Spread 1 cup cottage or ricotta cheese evenly over crust. Set aside.

Filling: Brown meat in a large skillet; drain fat. Stir in onion, garlic, green pepper, tomatoes with their liquid, tomato paste, sugar, oregano and salt. Pour over cottage cheese in crust.

Bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Bake 3 or 4 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
