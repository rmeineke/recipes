Scallopini w/ Goat Cheese

Ingredients

  • Veal scallopini meat
  • Goat cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Garlic
  • Tomato or Cream Sauce

Directions

On Scallopini meat, rub with garlic and layer a basil leaf and small amount of goat cheese. Serve with sauce.
