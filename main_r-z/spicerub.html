Spice-rubbed London Broil

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 - 2 lb London Broil

Directions

In a small bowl, combine cumin, sugar, salt, pepper and cayenne, stirring to blend evenly. Sprinkle the mix evenly over both sides of the meat. Cover and refrigerate until needed.

Grill or broil the meat for about six minutes per side or until meat is done to desired tastes. Let stand ten minutes before carving.
