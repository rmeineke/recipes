Ranch Taco Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 1 tbs chili powder
  • 1 - 16 oz package salad greens
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 8 oz salsa
  • 8 oz Ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips

Directions

Cook chicken, oil and chili powder in large skillet until chicken is cooked through. Toss chicken, greens and cheese in large bowl. Top with salsa, dressing and chips.
