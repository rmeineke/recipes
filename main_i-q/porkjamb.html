Pork Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork, cubed or slices 1/4" thick
  • 4 hot links, spicy sausage or similar
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped red pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 3 whole cloves garlic
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups uncooked rice (no Minute rice)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

1. Brown pork and sausage with olive oil in Dutch oven or very large covered pan 10-15 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste. Drain fat.

2. Add onion, peppers, celery, tomatoes, garlic and water to the meat. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes on medium low heat.

3. Add rice, mix well. Add tabasco to taste (I used about 1 1/4 ounce and it was very spicy)

4. Cover and allow entire mixture to simmer on medium heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. If rice is not quite cooked, allow a few more minutes on the heat.

Serves: 4-6
