Louise’s Wild Rice Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey or beef
  • 1 cup wild rice, cooked according to instructions
  • 6 ounces fresh, sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can bamboo shoots
  • 1 can sliced water chestnuts
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery

Directions

Saute onion and celery. Add meat and brown. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly. Bake in a large covered casserole dish for 45 minutes at 350°.
