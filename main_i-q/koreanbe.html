Korean BBQ Beef

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef, sliced as thin as as humanly possible. Tri-tip works well.
  • Marinade:
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1 bunch green onions, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry sherry
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seed oil
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds

Directions

Combine ingredients for marinade. Marinate beef for 15-20 minutes. Quickly grill on a very hot babrbecue.
