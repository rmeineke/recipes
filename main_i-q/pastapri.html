Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

  • 3 cups broccoli buds
  • 3 asparagus spears cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tomato, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon basil
  • 8 ounces linguine

Directions

Steam broccoli and asparagus until tender crisp, drain and keep warm. Heat oil in large skillet and saute garlic. Add mushrooms and tomato, cook 2 minutes. Stir in cream, cheese, basil, broccoli and asparagus, heat through. Cook linguine according to package directions, drain and return to pot. Pour sauce over hot linguine, toss quickly and serve.
