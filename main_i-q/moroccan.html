Moroccan Vegetable Stew

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 medium coarsely chopped zucchini
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 1 large chopped carrot
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons each ground ginger and cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 (15-19 ounce) cans rinse and drained chickpeas or garbanzo beans
  • 1 (14-1/2 ounce) can stewed tomatoes with juice
  • **I added one jar of chopped up marinated artichoke hearts

Directions

Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add zucchini, onion, carrot and garlic; cook 5 minutes. Stir ein broth, ginger, cumin, coriander, cinnamon and salt and pepper and simmer 8 minutes or until vegetables are softened; stir occasionally.

Meanwhile cook couscous according to instructions.

Serve stew over couscous.
