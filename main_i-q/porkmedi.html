Pork Mediterranee

Ingredients

  • 4 large pork chops
  • 2 tbs cooking oil
  • 3/4 cup dry wine or sherry
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 4 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tbs cilantro, minced

Directions

Brown the pork in the oil in a heavy skillet. Combine 1/2 cup wine, cumin, garlic, salt and pepper and pour over the pork. Cover and simmer 25 minutes. Combine 1/4 cup wine, lemon slices and cilantro and pour over. Simmer, turning the meat several times, for 10 minutes. Remove meat to serving dish, thicken sauce and pour over it.
