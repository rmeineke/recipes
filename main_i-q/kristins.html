Kristin’s Caper Pasta

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 4-6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 bunch fresh basil, washed and chopped
  • 1 bottle capers, drained
  • 1 package fresh, angel hair pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

Cook pasta according to directions.

Meanwhile, in a saute pan, saute garlic in tablespoon of olive oil. Add tomatoes and let them cook down for few minutes. Add basil, capers and salt and pepper to taste. Cook until all ingredients are heated through. Add to the cooked pasta and mix well.
