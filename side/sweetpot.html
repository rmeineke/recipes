Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground clove (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • Orange juice to desired consistency.
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, or to taste

Directions

Boil potatoes, drain, then add spices, start beating, adding orange juice, add brown sugar to taste. Serve.
