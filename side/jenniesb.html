Jennie's Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds bacon ends, cup up small
  • 2 very large white onions, chopped
  • 4 large (1 pound 15 ounce) cans Van de Camp pork and beans and liquid
  • 1 - 14 ounce bottle catsup
  • 1 cup dark molasses
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 generous tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 3 tablespoons Worchestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

    Directions

    Fry bacon, add onions. Mix everything else in casserole dish, then add bacon. Bake covered at 300° for 3 hours.
