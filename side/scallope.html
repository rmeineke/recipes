Scalloped Corn

Ingredients

  • 1 can cream-style corn
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbl melted butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbl flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Pour into a greased casserole dish. Bake at 350° for 35 to 45 minutes.
