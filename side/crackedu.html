Cracked Bulgur Wheat

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cracked Bulgur wheat
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • Chopped garlic
  • 1 cup baby green peas
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

*Bulgur wheat can be found in the "fine food" section of the grocery store.

Directions

Soak wheat in water until saturated. Drain excess water. Set aside.

Saute onion and garlic in oil.

Mix expanded wheat into onions and continue cooking until excess moisture is cooked off. Salt and pepper to taste.

Stir in peas just prior to serving.
