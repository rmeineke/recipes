Asparagus Gruyere Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh asparagus
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp lemon-pepper or garlic salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme or tarragon, crushed
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyere or Swiss cheese

Directions

Wash asparagus and break off and discard woody bases. Cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces. Cook asparagus in a small amount of boiling water for 3 or 4 minutes, or until crisp-tender. Drain and set aside.

In a large skillet, cook onion and pepper in olive oil until soft, about 4 minutes over medium heat. Remove from heat and drain. Transfer to a 3-quart rectangular baking dish. Top with cooked asparagus. Cover with plastic wrap and chill.

In a large mixing bowl, combine eggs, milk, flour, Parmesan, lemon-pepper and thyme. Beat until smooth with wire whisk. Pour into a bowl, cover and chill.

The next day, preheat oven to 425°.

Prepare 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray or grease.

Pour egg mixture over layers in baking dish. Bake about 25-30 minutes, or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Sprinkle with shredded cheese and bake 3-5 minutes more, or until cheese is melted.
