Bow Ties w/ Lemon Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon flour
  • 2 (5-0z) boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • Olive oil cooking spray
  • 1 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 lb. bow tie pasta
  • 1 (10 ounce) bag prewashed spinach, trimmed, torn into bite size pieces
  • 1 medium onion,halved lengthwise, thinly sliced cross-wise
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoon each-chopped fresh thyme and unsalted butter

Directions

Place 1 tablespoon flour on sheet of waxed paper; coat chicken; shake off excess. With cooking spray,coat large nonstick skillet; heat over medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook, turning once, 4 minutes, until browned. Cover; reduce heat to medium-low; cook, turning occasionally, 5-7 minutes, until no longer pink at center. Remove to cutting board. Let cool 5 minutes. Slice 1/4 in. thick, halve slices lengthwise.

Meanwhile, in large pot, bring 4 quarts water and 1 teaspoon salt to a boil. Add past; cook according to package directions. Add spinach; cook 10 seconds, until wilsted. Drain; return to pot.

Wipe out skillet; with cooking spray, coast skillet; heat over medium-high heat.. Add onioon; cook, stirring occasionally, 7 minutes until weel borwwned and carmalized. Stir in 1 1/4 cups broth, the lemon zest and juice, garlic, pepper and remaining salt; simmer 2 minutes. Whisk together remaining 1/4 cup broth and remaining 1 tablespoon flour in small glass measure; stir into simmering mixture. Simmer, stirring, 2 minutes, until slightly thickened. Add thyme and butter, swirl skillet until butter melts. Stir in chicken. Remove from heat.

Pour chicken mixture over pasta and spinach in pot; toss to mix. Spoon into 4 pasta bowls.
