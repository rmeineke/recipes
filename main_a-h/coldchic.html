Cold Chicken/Rice Fruit Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of Uncle Ben's white rice in a pouch
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 stalks of celery, chopped finely
  • 1 can pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1 can mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 small package of slivered almonds
  • 1/3 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise

Directions

Boil chicken and cut into bite size pieces. Cook rice per package directions. Let everything cool. Add celery and fruit. Mix the ranch and mayonnaise together and add and coat all the mixture.
