Cilantro Stuffed Chicken with Poblano Chile Sauce and Rice Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless/boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups Poblano Chile Sauce
  • 3 cups Rice Salad

Directions

Preheat broiler and lightly brush a shallow baking pan with some oil. Finely chop garlic and in a small bowl stir together with cilantro and salt. Pat chicken dry and trim any fat.

To form a pocket in chicken for filling: Put a chicken breast half on a cutting board and, beginning in middle of one side of breast, horizontally insert a sharp thin knife 3/4 of the way through center, moving knife in a fanning motion to create a pocket. (Opening will only be about 1 inch wide) Make pockets in remaining chicken breats in same manner. Put about 1/2 tablespoon filling into each pocket and with finger spread evenly.

Transfer chicken to baking pan and brush with remaining oil. Season chicken with salt and pepper and broil 5-6 inches from heat 7 minutes. Turn chicken over and broil until just cooked through, 3-5 minutes more.

Serve ((some other stuff))

Serves 4.
