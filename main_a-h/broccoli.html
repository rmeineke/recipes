Broccoli Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes frozen broccoli spears, cooked and drained
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can golden mushroom soup
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup grated Velveeta cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Crushed Ritz crackers
  • Butter

Directions

Combine the first 6 ingredients and stir well. Pour into a greased 9x13 pan and top with crackers, dot with butter. Bake at 350° for about an hour.
