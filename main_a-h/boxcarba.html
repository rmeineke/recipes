Boxcar Barbecue

Ingredients

  • 6 lb Boston butt pork roast
  • 1 (16-ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 (5-ounce) bottle soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 (5-ounce) bottle Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp dry minced onion

Directions

Place roast in roasting pan. Mix all other ingredients and pour over roast. Bake at 300° for 4 to 5 hours. Remove all bones and shred meat.
