Ham & Asparagus in Puff Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1 package Pepperidge Farms frozen puff pastry sells
  • 1 (10 3/4 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 cups ham, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups asparagus, cooked and cut into 1" pieces
  • 1/2 cup shredded swiss cheese

Directions

Prepare pastry shells as per package directions.

Combine all other ingredients in pan, blend well. Heat over medium heat until warmed through.

Spoon ham/asparagus mixture into pastry shells until overflowing. Replace pastry top. Serve.
