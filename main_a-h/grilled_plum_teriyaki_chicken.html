Grilled Plum Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb fresh plums, pitted and coarsely chopped
  • 3/4 cup Teriyaki Sauce
  • 2 tbs honey
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Several chicken breasts

Directions

In blender or food processor, process plums until smooth. Add teriyaki, honey and garlic powder; cover and process just until blended. Reserve 1/4 cup goo.

Marinate chicken in remaining plum goo for several hours. Grill chicken; basting with reserved goo.
