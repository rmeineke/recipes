Chicken & Shitake Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, boned and slices
  • 2 extra lean pork sausages, sliced
  • Shitake mushrooms, stems removed and halved or several large regular mushrooms
  • Ginger, julienned
  • 2 green onions, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup corn starch
  • 1/4 cup dry sherry or any white wine
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions

Mix first five ingredients together. Add remainder of ingredients and mix thoroughly. Steam or wok the entire mixture until cooked.

Serves: 4
