Creamy Pork and Apple Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs boneless pork roast (or lamb or beef) cut into 1 1/2 to 2 inch chunks, trimmed of fat
  • 2 cups broth
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Directions

Put all ingredients in a 4-5 quart pot, bring to boil, cover tightly, reduce heat and simmer 2 1/2 hours or until meat is tender. Add the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound think skinned potatoes, scrubbed and cut in 1 inch chunks (3 cups)
  • 1 large Granny Smith apple,peeled,cored and thinly slice
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 cup milk whisked with 2 tablespoons flour
