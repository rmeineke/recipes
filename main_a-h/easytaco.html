Easy Taco Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • Water
  • Tostada corn tortillas
  • Sliced olives
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Grated cheese
  • Frozen corn

Directions

Brown the ground beef and onion then drain. Replace in pan and add taco seasoning and water as per package directions. Remove from heat and cool for 30 minutes.

Layer 1/2 of the meat, 1/2 the sliced olives, 1/4 head shredded lettuce, 1/2 the cheese and 1/2 cup corn. Repeat the layering stopping with the layer of cheese.

Bake for 45 minutes at 350°
