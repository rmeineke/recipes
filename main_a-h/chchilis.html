Chicken Chili Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 - 7 ounce can chopped green chiles
  • 1 - 14 1/2 ounce can pinto beans, drained
  • 2 - 14 1/2 ounce cans ready cut tomatoes
  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup salsa
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Garnish:
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Cilantro
  • Chopped green onion
  • Chopped olives

Directions

In a large pan, saute chicken pieces until cooked through.

Add remainder of ingredients and simmer for twenty minutes. Serve with garnish.

Serves: 4
