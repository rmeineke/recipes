Hot Artichoke Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 (14 oz) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Directions:

Stir together and bake at 350° for 30 mins. Serve with French bread pieces.
