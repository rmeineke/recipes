Yogurt Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups yogurt, drained
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons dill
  • 1 medium cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:

Combine ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate overnight. Serve with cubed french bread or similar.
