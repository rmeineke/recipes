Habañero Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, chopped
  • 1 large (approximately 1" long) habañero pepper, finely chopped
  • **** Be sure to use gloves as it burns!
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • Juice from 2-3 limes
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

Mix all ingredients together. Serve with chips.
