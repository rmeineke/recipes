Spinach Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 2 cans sliced water chestnuts, chopped up
  • 2 packages Knorr’s Leek soup mix
  • 1 loaf round French bread

Directions:

Mix together first 4 ingredients and let refrigerate for at least a couple of hours to soften soup mix and let flavors mingle.

Hollow out bread and place dip inside.
