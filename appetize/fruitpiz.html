Fruit Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups corn flakes cereal crushed (1 cup crushed)
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 2 tbs butter, at room temperature
  • 2 tbs light corn syrup
  • 1 lb cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 jar (about 7 ounces) marshmallow creme
  • 3 cups sliced fruit

Directions:

Heat oven to 350. Spray 12-inch pizza pan with non-stick cooking spray.

Mix corn flakes, sugar, butter and corn syrup in a bowl. Press mixture evenly over bottom of prepared pizza pan.

Bake for 5 to 8 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool completely on wire rack.

Mix cream cheese and marshmallow cream in a bowl. Spread over crust, leaving 1-inch border. Arrange fruit over top and refrigerate for at least one hour.

Cut into slices and serve.
