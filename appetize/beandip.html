Bean Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 large can refried beans
  • 1 can white chunk chicken (or fresh cooked)
  • 1 small can diced green chiles
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Diced green onions
  • Chopped olives

Directions:

Mix beans, chicken, chiles, cover with shredded cheese and microwave until cheese is melted. Cover with sour cream and diced green onions and olives. Serve with tortilla chips.
