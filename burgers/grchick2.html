Grilled Chicken Olivada Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Olivada:
  • 1/2 cup pitted, green olives
  • 2/3 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lime peel
  • [Optional] Add some chopped jalapeno for a bit of heat
  • For the Chicken and Buns:
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced jalapeno chile
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast, pounded thin
  • 4 Kaiser or hard rolls, split
  • 4 large leaves romaine lettuce

Directions

Olivada: Combine Olivada ingredients in food processor and chop until the consistency of paste.

Heat pan or barbecue. Combine oil, jalapeno, garlic, pepper and 1/8 teaspoon salt in microwaveproof cup. Microwave on high for 30 seconds. Brush half of the oil mixture on the cut side of the rolls. Brush remaining oil on chicken; sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Grill chicken 4 - 5 minutes per side until cooked through. Grill rolls until lightly toasted.

Arrange lettuce on rolls; top with chicken. Spread Olivada over chicken and serve.
