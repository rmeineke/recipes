Yorkshire Pudding

Ingredients:

    ___ 2 eggs
    ___ 1 cup flour
    ___ 1 cup milk
    Pinch salt

Directions:

    Mix together well. Either cook with roast or heat 9x13 pan with 2 tablespoons butter first then pour in Yorkshire. Cook 45 mins to 1 hour at 425 degrees.
