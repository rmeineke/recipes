Cheese-Garlic Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 450°.

Stir ingredients until soft dough forms. Turn onto surface generously sprinkled with Bisquick. Knead 10 times. Roll out until 1/2 inch thick. Cut into 2 1/2 inch circles. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until golden brown.

Melt 2tbs butter and 1/8 tsp garlic powder and brsuh over warm biscuits.
