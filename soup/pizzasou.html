Pizza Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 (15-ounce) cans stewed Italian style tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can Italian style tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups thinly sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 lb pepperoni, sliced and then quartered
  • 1/2 lb sausage, browned
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/4 tsp ground red pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 (8-ounce) can sliced ripe olives
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarela cheese

Directions

Combine first three ingredients in a large sauce pan; cook over medium heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low; add next seven ingredients. Cook uncovered, 20 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat; stir in olives and green onions. Top with cheese.
