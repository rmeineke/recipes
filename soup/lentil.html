Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 2 quarts cold water
  • 1 cup trimmed, scraped and finely diced carrots
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup finely diced celery
  • 1 cup skinned, seeded and chopped ripe tomato
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

In a large soup pot combine all ingredients except salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer and cook, covered, 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally or until lentils are very tender. Remove bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.

Serves 10.
