Zuppa Fresca dell Orzo


Ingredients:

    ___ 1 cup diced pink skinned potatoes, unpeeled
    ___ 1/2 cup diagonally sliced celery
    ___ 1/2 cup diagonally sliced carrot
    ___ 2 garlic cloves, minced
    ___ 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
    ___ 8 cups chicken stock, fat skimmed from surface
    ___ 1/4 teaspoon saffron (optional)
    ___ 1 cup diagonally cut fresh green beans
    ___ 1/2 cup baby lima beans, fresh or frozen
    ___ 3 tablespoons orzo or riso (rice-shaped pasta)
    ___ 1 cup coarsely chopped hearts of escarole or collard
    ___ greens
    ___ 1 cup zucchini or yellow squars, quartered lengthwise
    ___ then cut diagonally
    ___ 1/2 cup diagonally sliced asparagus spears
    ___ 1/2 cup diagonally sliced scallions
    ___ 1/2 cup tiny peas, fresh thawed frozen
    ___ 1/3 cup diced plum tomatoes
    ___ Salt pepper and Parmesan cheese to taste.
    ___

Directions:

1. Combine potato,celery,carrot,garlic and olive oil
in large broad saucepan. Cover, cook over low heat,
stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender,
not browned, about 10 minutes.

2. Add broth and saffron. Heat to boiling. Stir
in green beans, lima bean, orzo. Cook, stirring until
tender, about 8 minutes. Add escarole or greens, simmer
5 minutes.

3. Stir in zucchini or yellow squash, asparagus, scallions,
green peas. Simmer just until tender and heated through,
about 5 minutes. Add tomatoes. Salt and pepper to
taste. Ladle into bowls. Served with Parmesan if
desired. (I don't). Serves 6-8
