Chicken Corn Chowder Ole

  • 1 teaspoon butter or margarine
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 pounds red potatoes cut into 1/2" diced
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 cups (fat free) half and half or 2% milk
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • (I used Jarlsberg)
  • 2 * 10-12 ounce cans chicken breast
  • (I boiled my own and diced up)
  • 2 * 14 ounce cans cream style corn
  • 1 * 4 ounce can of diced green chiles with juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • chopped cilantro
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Combine butter or margarine, onion, potatoes and garlic and microwave on high for appx. 6 minutes or until softened. Spoon into a Dutch oven and add half & half or milk, cheese, chicken, corn, chilies with liquid, hot sauce, salt, pepper and cumin and heat on low, stirring often, for 15 minutes. Stir in cilantro and serve immediately.
