Broccoli Cheese Soup


Ingredients:

    ___ 3 cups water
    ___ 1 pound chopped broccoli
    ___ 3 chicken bouillon cubes
    ___ 1 chopped onion

    White sauce:
    ___ 1/4 pound butter or margarine
    ___ 4-5tablespoon flour
    ___ 2 cups milk
    ___ Salt and pepper to taste
    ___ 1-8 ounce cream cheese-cubed

Directions:

Simmer first 4 ingredients until tender.
Make sauce, adding cream cheese at the end and heat until
cheese is melted. Add to broccoli mixture. Mix
thoroughly and serve.
