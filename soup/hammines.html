Hamburger Minestrone Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can beans
  • 2 cans corn
  • 1 28 ounce can chopped tomatoes
  • 1-2 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 pound carrots, sliced
  • 3-4 celery stalks sliced
  • 2-2 1/2 pounds elbow macaroni or other small egg noodle
  • garlic
  • Italian seasoning to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Brown ground beef and onions. Add all other ingredients and cook til done.
